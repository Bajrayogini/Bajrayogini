About Us

Bajrayogini Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting spiritual and social welfare through various initiatives aligned with vedic traditions.

Our Objectives

  • Spread spiritual awareness of goddess Ugratara Vajrayogini.
  • Promote well-being through yoga and meditation.
  • Construct and preserve religious sites.
  • Promote religious literatures and arts.
  • Raise awareness about religious tolerance.
  • Support ancient science and technology.
  • Research and produce Ayurvedic medicines.
  • Enhance cultural understanding and preservation.
  • Engage in social welfare initiatives.
  • Foster global cooperation for societal development.

Bajrayogini Foundation is committed to achieving these objectives through dedicated efforts and collaborations with communities and stakeholders.
